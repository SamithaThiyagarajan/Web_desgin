Today's Astronomical Events

Solar Eclipse

There will be a partial solar eclipse visible in parts of Europe and Africa today. The eclipse will reach its peak at 12:30 PM UTC.

Meteor Shower

The Perseids meteor shower will peak tonight, offering a stunning display of shooting stars. The best viewing time will be between midnight and dawn.

Planetary Conjunction

A rare alignment of Jupiter and Saturn will be visible just after sunset in the western sky. The planets will appear very close to each other.

Comet Observation

Comet C/2023 T2 will be visible in the night sky. Bring your telescope for a closer look at this icy traveler from the outer solar system.
