Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet and closest to the Sun. It has a rocky surface and no atmosphere.

Venus

Venus has a thick atmosphere of carbon dioxide and sulfuric acid clouds, making it the hottest planet.

Earth

Earth is the only planet known to support life. It has a diverse climate and liquid water on its surface.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet, Mars has the tallest volcano and the largest dust storms in the solar system.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet with a strong magnetic field and dozens of moons, including Ganymede.

Saturn

Saturn is best known for its stunning ring system and has over 80 known moons.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side and has a faint ring system. It is an ice giant with a cold atmosphere.

Neptune

Neptune is known for its deep blue color, strong winds, and the Great Dark Spot, a massive storm.
